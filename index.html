Get to know me

I design for a better experience.

My name is Walai. I am a creative and skilled graphic designer born in Thailand and currently living in Aarhus, Denmark. I have been a digital artist working with TV-commercials, feather films and visual media marketing in the post-production industry. My curiosity and willingness to learn drive me to web development path.
From the background in creating outstanding visual to the path of user experience design, I have discovered that a design is more than just beautiful but it is also about how people utilize it. I specialize in designing webpage, application prototype, visual graphic, and showcase mockup.
